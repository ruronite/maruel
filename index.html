About Maruel

Maruel is a reputable business entity established in 2019 and officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC). At Maruel, we are committed to making a positive impact in the food industry. Our core service revolves around food processing.

Our Mission:

At Maruel, our mission is simple yet profound: to produce food products of unparalleled quality and healthiness. We firmly believe that what you consume plays a vital role in your well-being, and we are dedicated to providing you with the finest, most nutritious options available.

Our Vision:

Our vision at Maruel is to become the preferred brand in every household. We aspire to be synonymous with excellence, trustworthiness, and a commitment to health and well-being. We aim to achieve this by consistently delivering top-notch products that cater to the diverse needs of our valued customers

Ferzima: Our Trademark

Ferzima stands as a registered trademark under the Maruel company umbrella. It represents our unwavering dedication to quality and authenticity in every product we offer. When you see Ferzima, you can trust that you are choosing a brand that prioritizes your health and satisfaction.

At Maruel, we are not just producing food; we are crafting experiences, nourishing lives, and building a legacy of excellence. Join us on our journey towards a healthier, tastier future for all.
